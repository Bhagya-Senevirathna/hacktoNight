Our University Events

HACKTO-NIGHT, an overnight hackathon organized by the FOSS Community of NSBM, was successfully unfolded on the 14th and 15th of October 2022, at the Faculty of Computing.

Comprising over 100 competitors in participation, HACKTO-NIGHT mainly intended to increase NSBM undergraduates' contribution toward open source and uplift their technological knowledge, problem-solving skills, coding skills, and team spirit.

The hackathon was glamoured with the graceful participation of Prof. Chaminda Rathnayake, Deputy-Vice Chancellor, Dr. Rasika Ranaweera - Dean, Faculty of Computing, Mr. Mohomed Shafraz - Master in charge of the FOSS Community of NSBM, Mrs. Manoja Weerasekara - Senior lecturer of the Faculty of Computing, Mr. Naji Saravanapavan - Senior lecturer of Faculty of Computing and Mr. Pramudya Tilakaratne - Lecturer of Faculty of Computing.

NSBM Green University, concluded the largest university level IEEE Day celebration in Sri Lanka today on a successful note.

Creating an open platform for knowledge sharing on modern technologies and their effect on innovation, the celebration was organized by the university's IEEE Student Branch under the theme "Leverage technology for a better tomorrow".

The event was graced by the presence of Prof. Chaminda Rathnayake, Deputy Vice Chancellor of NSBM Green University, Prof. Baratha Dodankotuwa, Head - Academic Development and Quality Assurance, Deans of the faculties, academics of NSBM and many industry experts representing leading Sri Lankan corporates including OREL IT, Virtusa and Aeturnum.
